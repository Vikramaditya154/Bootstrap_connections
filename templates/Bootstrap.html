CONTENT DELIVERY NETWORK(CDN)

Bootstrap CDN is the distribution of the most popular CSS framework in the world using a content delivery network.
It is a geographically distributed group of servers that work together to provide fast delivery of Internet content.
CDN enables a quick way to serve files over the internet such as HTML, Javascript, CSS, images, and videos.
